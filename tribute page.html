The life and political journey of Sushma ..

  • 1952-Born in Ambala Cant.pursed law at Punjab university,Chandigarh

  • 1973-started pratcice as an advocate in SC.

  • 1977-Elected to Harayana legislative assembly;became youngest cabinet minster

  • 1990- Elected to Rajya Sabha

  • 1996-Elected Lok Sabha MP from South Delhi constituency

  • 1998-Sworn in as Union minster for I&B and telecom ;film production gets industry status

  • oct 1998-Became first female CM of Delhi

  • 2000-Returned to Rajya Sabha made Cabine minister(I&B)

  • 2003-Sworn in as Cabinet minister for Health,family ,welfare& Parliamentary Affairs

  • 2009-Elected Lok Sabha MP from Vidisha constituency; became leader of opposition

  • 2014-Elected to 16th Lok Sabha ;took charge as Minster of External affairs

  • 2016-Kidney transplant at AIIMS,Delhi.

  • Aug 6 2019-Passed away;survived by husband Swaraj Kaushal and daugther Bansuri
